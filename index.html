ABOUT ME

Hi! My name is Mark. I'm from Naga City, Philippines. I am a career-shifter and I love creating my own stuff. I was a former Marketing Officer at Camella Communities, the country's largest home developer. There, I was able to develop my management skills and most importantly, I was engaged and creative. Aside from scribbling down ideas, I enjoy solving problems. Whenever I make a project, I ask myself if whether I am able to improve or solve an existing problem out of the things that I create.

I've always thought that thru computer programming, one can become a difference maker. That's why after 2.5 years, I decided to quit my job to pursue what I consider a long-time "creative itch". I enrolled in Tuitt Coding Bootcamp Philippines, wherein students would undergo intensive IT training to become a full-stack web developer within 3 months. From a former marketing guy who hasn't written a single line of computer code before, here I am, making my own stuff and doing something I am truly passionate about.
